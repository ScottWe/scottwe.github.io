# Education

---

#### Undergrad Education (Sept. 2014 -- Apr. 2019)

* **School:** University of Waterloo
* **Degree:** Bachelor of Mathematics (BMath)
* **Major:** Computer Science
* **Details:** Honours with Distinction, Co-operative Education Program

I attended the University of Waterloo from September 2014 to April 2019.
My original degree program was for a Bachelor of Computer Science and my interests were in artificial intelligence.
As I was exposed to more mathematics and theoretical computer science, I switched my degree to a Bachelor of Mathematics and began learning about formal methods.
Through these courses I met Dr. Arie Gurfinkel.

#### Masters Education (Sept. 2019 -- Aug. 2021)

* **School:** University of Waterloo
* **Degree:** Master of Mathematics (MMath)
* **Research Area:** Computer Science
* **Thesis:** [Smart Contract Analysis Through Communication Abstractions](master.pdf)

I continued my education at the University of Waterloo from September 2019 to August 2021 as a masters student.
I worked with Dr. Arie Gurfinkel and Dr. Richard Trefler on automated verification of smart contract programs.
This introduced me to parameterized verification and the verification of concurrent systems.

#### Doctoral Education (Sept. 2021 -- Present)

* **School:** Dalhousie University
* **Degree:** Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)
* **Research Area:** Mathmatics
* **Thesis:** Ongoing (categorical methods in quantum program analysis)

In September 2021 I moved to Dalhousie University to work with Dr. Neil J. Ross on formal methods in quantum computing.
I started out working on equational theories for Toffoli-Hadamard circuits.
This eventually lead to the study of control-flow in superposition and parameterized quantum circuits through the lens of category theory.
